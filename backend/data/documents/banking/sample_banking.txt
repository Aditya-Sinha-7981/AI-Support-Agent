# Sample banking knowledge (development)

## Savings accounts
The minimum opening deposit for an Everyday Savings account is 500 US dollars.
Customers must provide a government-issued photo ID and proof of address dated within the last 90 days.

## Wire transfers
Domestic wire transfer requests received before 2:00 PM Eastern Time on a business day
are typically processed the same day. International wires may take two to five business days.
