# Sample e-commerce policy (development)

## Returns and refunds
Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund if they are unused,
in original packaging, and include all accessories. Final sale items are marked at checkout and cannot be returned.

## Shipping
Standard shipping to addresses in the continental United States usually takes 5 to 7 business days.
Express shipping options are available at checkout where offered.

Global Horizon Retail - E-commerce Policies and Customer Support Guidelines
Effective Date: March 1, 2026

1. Shipping and Delivery Policies
We strive to process and ship all orders as quickly as possible. Shipping times begin once the order leaves our fulfillment center, not from the moment the order is placed.

Processing Time: All in-stock orders are processed within 1 to 2 business days. Orders placed after 2:00 PM Eastern Time on Friday will begin processing on the following Monday.

Domestic Shipping Options (Contiguous United States):
- Standard Shipping: Takes 5 to 7 business days. Cost is $5.99. Free for all orders over $50.00.
- Expedited 2-Day Shipping: Takes 2 business days. Cost is $14.99.
- Overnight Shipping: Delivered the next business day. Cost is $29.99. Cut-off time for Overnight shipping is 12:00 PM Eastern Time.

International Shipping:
- We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International Standard delivery takes 10 to 21 business days.
- International Express delivery takes 4 to 7 business days.
- Customs, Duties, and Taxes: The customer is solely responsible for all customs duties, foreign taxes, or other fees associated with international shipments. These fees are not included in the checkout price and are collected by the local courier upon delivery.

Lost or Stolen Packages:
- If tracking shows "Delivered" but the customer cannot locate the package, they must wait 48 hours before filing a claim, as carriers sometimes scan packages prematurely.
- If the package is still missing after 48 hours, the customer must contact support within 7 days to initiate a lost package investigation.


2. Returns, Refunds, and Exchanges
We want our customers to be completely satisfied with their purchases.

Standard Return Policy:
- Most physical items can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund.
- Items must be unused, unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.
- Electronics and appliances must include all cords, manuals, and original accessories.

Exceptions and Non-Returnable Items:
- Final Sale Items: Items explicitly marked as "Final Sale" at the time of purchase cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstances.
- Hygiene and Personal Care: For health and safety reasons, opened headphones, earbuds, undergarments, swimwear, cosmetics, and skincare products cannot be returned once the protective seal is broken.
- Gift Cards: Digital and physical gift cards are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash.

Restocking Fees:
- A 15% restocking fee applies to all opened laptops, desktop computers, drones, and televisions. This fee is deducted from the final refund amount.
- There is no restocking fee if the electronics are returned completely unopened and factory-sealed.

Return Process and Fees:
- Customers must initiate a return through the online Returns Portal to generate a pre-paid shipping label.
- A flat fee of $6.99 is deducted from the refund for the cost of return shipping. This fee is waived if the item arrived damaged or the wrong item was shipped.

Refund Timeline:
- Once the returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse, refunds are processed within 3 to 5 business days.
- It may take an additional 5 to 10 business days for the funds to appear on the customer's credit card statement, depending on the financial institution.


3. Accepted Payment Methods and Billing
We offer a secure checkout experience supporting multiple global payment gateways.

Accepted Credit and Debit Cards:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
- The billing address provided at checkout must exactly match the address on file with the credit card issuer to prevent fraud declines.

Digital Wallets:
- We accept PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. 
- When checking out with PayPal, the shipping address must be confirmed on the PayPal account.

Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL):
- We partner with Klarna and Affirm to offer split payments.
- Klarna Pay in 4: Splits the purchase into four equal, interest-free installments charged every two weeks.
- Affirm Monthly: Available for orders over $250. Offers monthly financing up to 12 months. Subject to credit check and approval by Affirm.

Order Modifications and Cancellations:
- Due to automated warehouse processing, orders can only be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placement.
- After 1 hour, the order is locked for shipping. Customers will need to receive the item and process a standard return.


4. Warranty Information and Repair Services
We stand behind the quality of the products we sell.

Standard Manufacturer Warranty:
- All new electronics, appliances, and power tools include a 1-Year Limited Manufacturer Warranty from the date of purchase.
- The limited warranty covers internal hardware failures and manufacturing defects.
- What is NOT covered: Accidental damage (drops, cracked screens, water damage), theft, loss, cosmetic wear and tear, and damage caused by unauthorized third-party repairs.

Extended Protection Plans (Horizon Protect):
- Customers can purchase an extended 2-year or 3-year Horizon Protect plan at checkout for eligible electronics.
- Horizon Protect covers accidental damage (including spills and cracked screens) starting from day one, and extends the mechanical failure coverage after the manufacturer warranty expires.
- Filing a Claim: Customers must register their product online. Claims can be filed 24/7 through the warranty portal. A $49 deductible applies to all accidental damage claims.


5. General Product FAQs and Store Policies
Information regarding pricing, inventory, and compatibility.

Price Match Guarantee:
- We will match the price of identical, in-stock items shipped and sold by major competitors (Amazon, Best Buy, Walmart, Target) within 14 days of purchase.
- The item must be exactly the same model, color, and condition. We do not match prices from third-party marketplace sellers or auction sites.

Out of Stock and Backorders:
- If an item is listed as "Backordered," it means the item is currently out of stock but a new shipment is on the way.
- Customers can place a backorder to reserve the item. The customer's credit card will not be charged until the item actually ships.

Apparel Sizing:
- Sizing runs standard for the US market. A comprehensive sizing chart is available on every clothing product page. If a customer is between sizes, we recommend ordering the larger size.


6. Customer Privacy and Data Security
Protecting customer data is our highest priority.

Data Collection:
- We collect standard information required to fulfill orders: Name, shipping address, billing address, email, and phone number.
- We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. All transactions are tokenized and processed by a secure third-party payment gateway (Stripe).

Account Deletion:
- Customers can request to have their account and personal data permanently deleted in compliance with CCPA and GDPR regulations. 
- Deletion requests must be submitted through the Privacy Portal at the bottom of our website and take up to 30 days to process. Note that we are legally required to retain basic transaction records for tax purposes.

7. Horizon Rewards Loyalty Program
Our loyalty program is completely free to join and rewards customers for their continued business.

Earning Points:
- Members earn 10 points for every $1.00 spent on eligible merchandise.
- Points are not earned on shipping fees, taxes, or the purchase of gift cards.
- Bonus points are frequently offered during special promotional events (e.g., Double Points Weekends).

Membership Tiers:
- Silver Tier (0 - 4,999 points annually): Free standard shipping on orders over $50.
- Gold Tier (5,000 - 14,999 points annually): Free standard shipping on all orders, 5% discount on all Horizon brand products, and early access to sales.
- Platinum Tier (15,000+ points annually): Free expedited 2-day shipping on all orders, 10% discount on all Horizon brand products, a dedicated customer service hotline, and an annual birthday gift.

Redeeming Points:
- Every 1,000 points can be redeemed for a $5.00 reward certificate at checkout.
- Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity (no purchases or point redemptions).


8. Subscribe and Save Program (Auto-Delivery)
For consumable products like coffee, supplements, and pet food, we offer a convenient auto-delivery subscription service.

Subscription Benefits:
- Customers receive an automatic 15% discount on the current retail price of all subscribed items.
- Free standard shipping is included on all Subscribe and Save deliveries, regardless of the order total.

Managing Frequencies:
- Delivery frequencies can be set to every 2 weeks, 4 weeks, 6 weeks, or 8 weeks.
- Customers can pause, skip a delivery, or change the delivery frequency at any time through the "My Subscriptions" dashboard.
- Any modifications must be made at least 48 hours before the next scheduled processing date.

Cancellations:
- There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Customers can cancel their subscription at any time.
- If an order has already entered the "Processing" phase, the cancellation will apply to the following scheduled delivery.


9. Wholesale and Corporate Purchases (B2B)
Global Horizon Retail supports business customers with bulk purchasing options and dedicated account management.

Volume Discounts:
- Orders exceeding $5,000 in a single transaction qualify for a 10% bulk discount.
- Orders exceeding $15,000 qualify for a customized quote provided by our B2B sales team.
- Volume discounts cannot be combined with standard promotional codes or holiday sales.

Tax-Exempt Orders:
- Non-profit organizations, resellers, and government entities can make tax-exempt purchases.
- To qualify, the organization must create a Corporate Account and upload a valid state-issued Tax Exemption Certificate.
- Tax exemption cannot be applied retroactively to past orders. The certificate must be approved prior to checkout.

Payment Terms:
- Approved corporate accounts may be eligible for Net-30 payment terms, allowing them to pay via invoice 30 days after the items have shipped.


10. Promotions, Coupon Codes, and Gift Orders
Rules regarding special offers and gifting to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

Promo Code Stacking:
- Only one promotional code can be applied per order. Customers cannot "stack" multiple discount codes.
- If a customer has a site-wide sale discount and a unique promo code, the checkout system will automatically apply whichever discount provides the greatest savings.

Gift Orders:
- Customers can select "This is a gift" at checkout to receive a gift receipt.
- Gift receipts hide the price of the items but include an order number so the recipient can process an exchange or return for store credit.
- Optional gift wrapping is available for a flat fee of $4.99 per item. Large items like appliances and televisions cannot be gift-wrapped.

Price Adjustments:
- If an item is purchased at full price and goes on sale within 7 days of the purchase date, we will issue a one-time price adjustment for the difference.
- Price adjustments are issued as store credit only and must be requested via customer support.


11. Sustainability and Ethical Sourcing Pledges
We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint and ensuring ethical supply chains.

Eco-Friendly Packaging:
- By 2026, 90% of our shipping boxes are made from 100% post-consumer recycled cardboard.
- We have phased out plastic bubble wrap in favor of biodegradable packing peanuts and corrugated paper wrap.

Carbon Offset Program:
- Customers have the option to add $0.99 to their order at checkout to offset the carbon emissions of their delivery.
- Global Horizon matches these contributions dollar-for-dollar, donating the funds to verified reforestation projects in North and South America.

Supplier Code of Conduct:
- All third-party manufacturers must sign our Supplier Code of Conduct, guaranteeing fair wages, safe working conditions, and zero tolerance for child or forced labor.
- We conduct independent, unannounced audits of our top 50 suppliers annually to ensure compliance.
